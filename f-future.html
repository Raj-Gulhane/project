Kisan Credit Card (KCC)

The Kisan Credit Card (KCC) scheme provides farmers with easy credit access at low interest rates to buy seeds, fertilizers, and equipment. This reduces their reliance on moneylenders, making credit accessible and affordable. KCC also includes accident insurance, ensuring additional security for farmers. The scheme not only supports daily agricultural needs but also helps in covering unforeseen expenses, improving farmers' financial independence.

Learn more about KCC

Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY)

PMFBY is a crop insurance scheme designed to shield farmers from crop losses caused by natural calamities, pests, and diseases. It ensures that farmers receive a fair compensation for losses, stabilizing their income and enhancing their resilience to environmental risks. By covering major risks, PMFBY helps farmers take up modern farming methods without fear of devastating losses.

Learn more about PMFBY

Soil Health Card Scheme

The Soil Health Card Scheme helps farmers monitor the health of their soil by providing nutrient status reports, enabling them to make informed decisions on soil management.

Learn more about Soil Health Card

eNAM - National Agriculture Market

The eNAM platform helps farmers get fair prices for their products by providing an online marketplace where they can sell their goods directly to buyers across the country.

Learn more about eNAM

PM-KMY - Pradhan Mantri Kisan Maan-Dhan Yojana

This scheme offers a pension to small and marginal farmers, ensuring financial security during old age by providing them a monthly pension after the age of 60.
